A Short History about Way Foods

Way Foods born 6 years ago when a group of people decided to join an anventure across United States and Canada, but they found a problem when during its journey didn't meet any affordable restaurant in the way. So, Cathlyn Meyer and Bernard Rochelle decided to buy groceries and build his own " way restaurant ", wich was the first name for Way Foods while the other travellers keep going across the United States and Canada with the time they started to becoming partners of the first founders and started the grwing of this restaurant chain that today have 268 headquarters across the world.
